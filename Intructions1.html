Instructions

  • Prepare the Dough: Mix rice flour, cumin seeds, carom seeds, red chili powder, and salt in a bowl. Gradually add water to form a smooth, pliable dough. Knead until soft and workable.
  • Shape the Dumplings: Grease your hands with oil. Pinch off small portions of dough and roll into smooth, round dumplings.
  • Steam the Dumplings: Prepare a steamer with boiling water and lightly grease the steamer basket. Arrange dumplings in the basket, ensuring they don't touch. Steam for 15-20 minutes until firm and translucent.
  • Serve the Dumplings: Cool slightly and serve warm with chutney or a spicy curry.
  • Optional Variations: Mix herbs like cilantro or mint into the dough. Stuff with a spiced filling before steaming for added flavor.

    • Enjoy your faraa!
